Types of Age-Related Macular Degeneration

Normal Retina

A healthy retina shows clear macular structure without abnormal deposits or vessel growth.

Dry AMD

Dry AMD occurs when the macula gradually thins and small protein deposits called drusen accumulate.

Wet AMD

Wet AMD happens when abnormal blood vessels grow under the retina and leak fluid.
